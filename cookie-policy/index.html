Τι Αντιπροσωπεύουν τα Cookies

Cookies είναι small text files που stored στο device σας όταν visit το Savaspin website. Αυτά τα files contain information που allows την website να recognize το device σας σε future visits και να remember τις preferences σας. Cookies είναι essential για proper functioning πολλών web features και improve significantly την user experience.

Πώς και Γιατί Χρησιμοποιούνται

Το Sava Spin uses cookies για various purposes που collectively enhance την platform experience. Technical cookies ensure proper site operation, preference cookies save τις settings σας like language και currency, analytics cookies help us understand usage patterns για improvements, και marketing cookies enable personalized offers και promotions relevant στα interests σας.

Τα Δικαιώματά Σας

Έχετε complete control over cookies stored από το Savaspin website. Μπορείτε να accept all cookies για full functionality, reject non-essential cookies με limited features, ή customize settings per category. Browser settings allow cookie deletion και blocking, though some features may not work properly without certain cookies.

Available options include:

  • Accept all cookies για optimal functionality

  • Reject non-essential cookies (limited features)

  • Customize per cookie category

  • Delete existing cookies via browser

  • Enable “Do Not Track” signal

  • Use private/incognito browsing mode

Note that disabling cookies may impact site functionality.
